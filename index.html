About Me



I am Luke Paolo B. Soraino, 21 years old, IT Student in PHINMA University of Pangasinan whose dream is to develop games.

With that in mind, it is also our job to inspire student programmers alike by sharing and teaching them profound knowledge that would surely aid our learning and future.
